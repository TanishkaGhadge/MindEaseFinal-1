# Meditation Audio Files

Place your meditation audio files in this folder with the following names:

- `om-chanting.mp3` - Om/Omkar chanting meditation audio
- `flute.mp3` - Calming flute meditation music
- `rain.mp3` - Rain sounds for relaxation

## Where to get free meditation audio:

1. **Pixabay** (https://pixabay.com/music/search/meditation/)
   - Free for commercial use
   - No attribution required
   - High quality audio

2. **Freesound** (https://freesound.org/)
   - Search for "om chanting", "meditation flute", "rain"
   - Free with Creative Commons licenses
   - May require attribution

3. **YouTube Audio Library**
   - Download meditation tracks
   - Convert to MP3
   - Free for use

## How to add files:

1. Download your chosen meditation audio files
2. Rename them to match the names above
3. Place them in this `public/sounds/` folder
4. Restart your development server
5. The audio will now play when you click the sound buttons
